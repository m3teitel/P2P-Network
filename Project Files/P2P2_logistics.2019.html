CPS 706 course project : A Peer to Peer shared photo album with distributed directory - Project Logistics

Grouping

  • You need to form a group of 2 to 3 students to implement the course project
  • We will collect the project submission on a group basis. Please elect a student representative within your group. The group representative will inform the TAs by email (a) the group composition (b) group representative information before or on  March 29, 2019. The group representative is in charge of submitting the project hand-in's.
  • After submission of group information TA/instructor will provide protocol port number for the student group.

Due Date

  • Your final project submission is due at 5pm, on 10.  April . It shall contain two parts: Java programs, and a project documentation. There shall be three programs for Directory Servers, P2P client, and P2P server respectively. Your documentation shall include the following: (a) user documentation: describes how to compile and run your programs, and (b) system documentation: describes data structures, design choices, and important algorithms used.

Hand-in

  • Mechnism of collection of Project source files and documentation files will be announced in early April.
  • Project documentation shall be in .pdf,.doc or .txt format.
  • Students will be requested to give demo to the TAs either during last week's labs or in specifically determined time slots. The schedule of demos will be made in the first week of April, and students should sign in for the demo before the demo time.
  • Demos will be conducted in lab slots on 11. April.
  • Any late submissions will incur 10% mark deduction per day. 

Plagiarism and disputes:

The project must be done independently by the project group. Copying from other groups is strictly prohibited. Students involved in plagiarism will get zero mark for the course project. Please put your code in secure place to prevent unauthorized plagiarism.

Disputes among the team members should be resolved on time, before final project submission. If project demo fails due to dispute among the members all members will be assigned grade which follows from the demo and code analysis.
